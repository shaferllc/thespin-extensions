# Add your publisher key

Your publisher key tells the exchange which account to credit for the lines you
show. It lives in the **Payouts** page at [thespin.ad](https://thespin.ad).

1. Open [thespin.ad](https://thespin.ad) and sign in.
2. Copy your key from **Payouts** (it looks like `thespin_…`).
3. Paste it into the `thespin.publisherKey` setting.

> Use the **Add your publisher key** button on the left to jump straight to the
> setting.

No key? The sponsored line still shows — it just earns nothing. Add the key any
time to start your **50%** revenue share.
