# Watch it in the status bar

Look at the bottom-right of the window. The 📣 item is the live sponsored line,
refreshed every few seconds straight from the exchange.

```
  📣 ★ Ramp · save time and money            ← top bid: ★ + highlighted
  📣 Linear · plan less, ship more           ← standard line
  📣 thespin · unclaimed                      ← nothing live; bid to claim it
```

- **★ and a highlighted background** mark the current top bid — the premium slot.
- **Click the line** to open the sponsor. That's a tracked click you earn on.
- **Hover** to see the brand, its price-per-1k, and your earning status.

Tune how often it refreshes with the `thespin.pollSeconds` setting. That's it —
you're earning from the wait.
